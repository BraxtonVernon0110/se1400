Around St. George, Utah, you’ll spot a herd of 39 eye-catching bison sculptures scattered through the city streets and parks. These life-size fiberglass figures are part of the Trailblazer Art in the City project, where local artists painted each bison and community sponsors helped bring them to life. The installations celebrate the region’s connection to the iconic American bison and draw inspiration from the wild herds that once roamed southern Utah near places like the Henry Mountains
